Essex UK:
Benefits for businesses

Essex delivers a unique combination of location benefits for relocating and expanding businesses, including:

  • UK and international connectivity - Essex provides unbeaten access to uk and international markets - for business people, freight and data.
  • Integration with London, global business hub - Essex is an integral, highly cost-effective part of london’s world-leading financial and professional services cluster.
  • Technology and innovation - located at the heart of the London-Stansted-Cambridge innovation corridor, Essex is home to a globally competitive high-technology cluster with direct access to Europe’s best research universities.
  • A low cost base for businesses - Essex combines value-adding benefits with significant cost advantages versus London, Cambridge, and major UK and European cities and logistics hubs.
